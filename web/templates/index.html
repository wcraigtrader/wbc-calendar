WBC 2025 Event Schedule

These calendars are provided on a 'best effort' basis, but are NOT the official schedule . The calendars will be updated as the schedule is updated, and as the program that generates the calendars from the schedule is improved. Please report any errors to wbc@trader.name .

If your browser handles 'webcal' files, click the button.
If you use GoogleCalendar, click the button.
Otherwise, copy the link from event name into your calendar program.

Download the scripts yourself at GitHub .
